What is the best way to recycle?

  • Buy recycled paper and print on both sides.
  • Recycle your outdated technology.
  • Make recycling bins readily available.
  • Recycle your empty ink and toner cartridges.
  • Buy remanufactured ink and toner cartridges.
  • Recycle old newspapers laying around the office.
  • Look for the recycled option in all the products you buy.
  • Buy rechargeable batteries.
  • Purchase rewritable CDs and DVDs so that you can reuse them from project to project.
  • Reuse your morning coffee cup.

What sort of objects can i not recycle?

  • Garbage.
  • Food waste.
  • Food-tainted items (such as: used paper plates or boxes, paper towels, or paper napkins).
  • Ceramics and kitchenware.
  • Windows and mirrors.
  • Plastic wrap.
  • Packing peanuts and bubble wrap.
  • Wax boxes.
  • Photographs
  • Medical waste
  • Polystyrene or styrofoam
  • Hazardous chemicals and chemical containers
  • Plastic toys or sporting goods equipment
  • Foam egg cartons
  • Wood
  • Light bulbs
  • Yard waste or garden tools
